FAQ

The important questions, answered directly.

What does Vonza do beyond website chat?

Vonza starts with the website front desk, but the paid workspace adds Today, Inbox, Calendar, Contacts, approval-first Automations, Customize, and Outcomes so the owner can operate from one place.

Does Vonza support Gmail and Google Calendar?

Yes, when Google is connected. Vonza can sync Gmail and Google Calendar into the operator workspace for review, drafts, scheduling context, and approval-first actions. If Google is not connected or not configured on a deployment, the rest of the product still degrades safely.

Does Vonza send replies or automations autonomously?

No. Vonza is approval-first. It can draft replies, follow-ups, campaigns, and some calendar actions, but the owner still reviews and approves sends or changes.

How are outcomes tracked?

Vonza tracks count-and-proof outcomes it can conservatively tie back to real routing, follow-up, campaign, booking, quote, checkout, or complaint-recovery paths. It does not claim full revenue attribution unless the proof path exists.

What setup is required?

At minimum, add your website, tune the front desk, and install the snippet. Connect Google if you want Inbox and Calendar live, then review Today, Contacts, and approval-first drafts as they appear.
