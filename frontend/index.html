FAQ

The important questions, answered directly.

What does Vonza do beyond website chat?

Vonza starts with the website front desk, but the paid workspace adds Today, Contacts, Customize, and Outcomes so the owner can operate from one place. Google-connected Inbox, Calendar, and Automations are optional beta for customers who want that deeper workflow.

Does Vonza support Gmail and Google Calendar?

Yes, as an optional beta when Google is connected and enabled on the deployment. Vonza can sync Gmail and Google Calendar for review, drafts, scheduling context, and approval-first actions. If Google is not connected, the stable front-desk core still works safely.

Does Vonza send replies or automations autonomously?

No. Vonza is approval-first. It can draft replies, follow-ups, campaigns, and some calendar actions, but the owner still reviews and approves sends or changes.

How are outcomes tracked?

Vonza tracks count-and-proof outcomes it can conservatively tie back to real routing, follow-up, campaign, booking, quote, checkout, or complaint-recovery paths. It does not claim full revenue attribution unless the proof path exists.

What setup is required?

At minimum, add your website, tune the front desk, import knowledge, and install the snippet. Then run a real test conversation and confirm the lead, contact, and outcome path. Connect Google later if you want the optional Inbox and Calendar beta.
